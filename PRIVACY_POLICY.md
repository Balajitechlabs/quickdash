# Privacy Policy — QuickDash

Last updated: July 2026 (Version 3.0 Update)

## What We Collect
QuickDash collects minimal anonymous telemetry to check installation health:
- **Device model** (e.g. "Pixel 8 Pro") — sent once on first install
- **Daily active ping** — an anonymous signal that the app was opened today
- **Crash reports** — stack trace details sent when a crash occurs

## What We Do NOT Collect
- **No Personal Details**: No names, emails, or personal identification markers are required or gathered.
- **On-Device Cryptographic Storage**: All UPI IDs, PayPal addresses, Quick Notes, and Clipboard history items are stored strictly in local Room SQL databases or SharedPreferences Datastore, encrypted on-device.
- **Local Biometric Security**: The optional Biometric Gate uses native Android authentication. Biometric templates never leave the device secure enclave.
- **Notification Listener Service logs**: The app logs and shows recent notifications only in your local left drawer. Zero notification text is exported or sent off-device.
- **Secure Screen Captures**: When Secure Mode is enabled, screenshots and app switcher window previews are blocked system-wide.

## How Data Is Transmitted
Anonymous installation telemetry pings are transmitted to a secure developer bot poller. No commercial third-party trackers are utilized.

## Contact
If you have questions, please reach out via GitHub Issues on the repository.
